From Surplus to Service, From Waste to Worth

FreshRescue bridges the gap between surplus food and hunger by connecting food providers with community organizations, offering a sustainable solution to reduce waste and feed those in need.

Join as Business Join as Organization

Powerful Features

Our integrated platform makes food rescue simple, efficient, and impactful for all participants.

Food Donation Platform

Restaurants, cafés, and food retailers can conveniently list their surplus food, specifying the quantity, type, and pickup details.

Organization Network

Partner organizations can view available food listings, claim donations, schedule pickups, and manage their inventory with ease.

Volunteer Management

Volunteers can sign up to assist with food pickups, transportation, and distribution, creating a reliable support network.

Food Rescue Tracker

A real-time dashboard showcases key metrics — including food rescued, organizations supported, and lives impacted through your contributions.

Rating System

Organizations and volunteers can rate and review each other, promoting accountability and building trust within our community.

Educational Resources

Access blog posts, videos, and tips on food waste reduction, sustainable practices, and healthy eating.

How It Works

Our platform makes food rescue simple and accessible for everyone involved.

1

Businesses Post Surplus

Food establishments list surplus items with details like quantity, type, and pickup time — making it easy to share what’s available.

2

Organizations Claim

Registered nonprofits and community groups browse listings and claim food that matches their needs — ensuring nothing goes to waste.

3

Volunteers Assist

When needed, our dedicated volunteer network steps up to assist with pickups and deliveries, bridging the gap between donors and recipients.

4

Food Reaches People

Claimed food is distributed by organizations to individuals and families across the community — turning surplus into support.

Benefits for Everyone

FreshRescue creates value for businesses, organizations, and the entire community.

Reduced Food Waste

Less food ends up in landfills, minimizing environmental impact and reducing greenhouse gas emissions.

Cost Savings

Increased Efficiency
